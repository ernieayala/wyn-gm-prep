## The Three-Act Hook

The legendary pirate [**Poppy von Barnacle**](gm_glossary.md#poppy-von-barnacle) founded [Rumplank](gm_glossary.md#rumplank) 200 years ago. Everyone thinks she was a swashbuckler. Truth: she was a war hero who beat the [**Cult of the Shivering Behemoth**](gm_glossary.md#cult-of-the-shivering-behemoth), then split their relic — [**the Jewel of the Indigo Isles**](gm_glossary.md#jewel-of-the-indigo-isles) — into four pieces and hid them across the islands.

The cult is rising again. The PCs hunt the jewel pieces, then defend Rumplank when the cult attacks.

> **GM-only:** Poppy is dead. The "treasure hunt" is really the PCs unknowingly assembling a cult relic. [Prismatic Colvi](gm_glossary.md#prismatic-colvi) (Ch 1 friendly priest) is a cultist. [Prince Kulupi](gm_glossary.md#prince-kulupi) (Ch 1 quest-giver) is the cult's mastermind, revealed in Ch 11.

## Act 1: Search for the Missing Map (Chapters 1–4)

The [Redfeather](gm_glossary.md#redfeathers) royals send the PCs to find Poppy's hidden hideout. Inside, they recover the first jewel piece — [**the Obsidian Star**](gm_glossary.md#obsidian-star).

- **Ch 1 — Edges of the Map.** Hunt the missing map half + a sapphire keystone in Rumplank, the wreck of the [Gasping Gipper](gm_glossary.md#gasping-gipper), and the [Writhing Swamp](gm_glossary.md#writhing-swamp). End: map assembled, but cursed and crumbling.
- **Ch 2 — "X" Marks the Spot.** Race to [**Jakapo Town**](gm_glossary.md#jakapo-town) ([galtzagorri](gm_glossary.md#galtzagorri) mushroom village) to undo the curse; deal with a twisted toymaker and a ruined amphitheater.
- **Ch 3 — What Makes the Mountain Shiver.** Reach [**Shiverskull Cave**](gm_glossary.md#shiverskull-cave) at the base of [Shivering Mountain](gm_glossary.md#shivering-mountain).
- **Ch 4 — Legacy of the Gilded Pirate.** Clear Poppy's proving grounds. Find the Obsidian Star + her journal. Discovery: there are three more jewel pieces.

**End-of-act party level:** 4.

## Act 2: Voyage to Runaway Reef (Chapters 5–8)

The PCs sail [Poppy's flagship](gm_glossary.md#chromatic-queen) across the [Indigo Isles](gm_glossary.md#indigo-isles), recovering the other three jewel fragments.

- **Ch 5 — Ghosts of Seaview.** [Bluebell Island](gm_glossary.md#bluebell-island). A botched ritual by Councilor Pelborii is haunting the harvest.
- **Ch 6 — Moonshadow Mysteries.** [Moonshadow Island](gm_glossary.md#moonshadow-island) has been overrun by Shadow Plane invaders; one of them stole the local jewel piece for fleshweaving experiments.
- **Ch 7 — The Flesh Stitcher's Fortress.** Defeat the fleshweaver, recover the fragment.
- **Ch 8 — Descent to Razorback Isle.** A sunken cult temple at [**Runaway Reef**](gm_glossary.md#runaway-reef). Water-elemental saboteurs are tearing it apart. Recover the final fragment.

**End-of-act party level:** 8.

## Act 3: Battle at Barnacle Bay (Chapters 9–12)

Triumphant return to Rumplank for the [**Founder's Day**](gm_glossary.md#founders-day) festival — then the cult strikes.

- **Ch 9 — Party in Rumplank.** Festival games and contests. Cult-hired arsonists set fires; cultists kidnap citizens (including the prince and princess) and steal the assembled Jewel.
- **Ch 10 — Where Few Dare to Tread.** Overland chase through the Okapi Hills and [salt desert](gm_glossary.md#crackbeak-salt-desert). Rescue some kidnapping victims. Travel through jungle to a hidden galtzagorri village led by the dragon [**Peerless Ruby**](gm_glossary.md#peerless-ruby).
- **Ch 11 — Cult of the Shivering Behemoth.** Infiltrate the cult's caverns. Arrive too late — the behemoth is reborn. **Reveal: Prince Kulupi is the cult mastermind.** Escape on Peerless Ruby's back.
- **Ch 12 — Battle for [Barnacle Bay](gm_glossary.md#barnacle-bay).** Defend Rumplank using resources gathered all campaign. Final fights: Prismatic Colvi, an undead Poppy, then Kulupi atop the behemoth.

**End-of-act party level:** 11.

## Cast Sheet — Names to Know

- [**Prince Kulupi**](gm_glossary.md#prince-kulupi) — gives the quest. *(Secret villain — Act 3.)*
- [**Princess Atriella**](gm_glossary.md#princess-atriella) — note-taker, helpful info.
- [**D'marti Tixima**](gm_glossary.md#dmarti-tixima) — chatty jungle explorer, finder of the original map half. Possible NPC companion.
- [**Headmistress U'oko**](gm_glossary.md#headmistress-uoko) — [Quill & Scroll Academy](gm_glossary.md#quill--scroll-academy), gives lore on [Poppy](gm_glossary.md#poppy-von-barnacle)'s old crew.
- [**Master Olo**](gm_glossary.md#master-olo) — [orpok](gm_glossary.md#orpok) martial-arts trainer. Optional combat training.
- [**Captain Nevergo**](gm_glossary.md#captain-nevergo) — surly apothecary, points to D'marti.
- [**Sagoya Pekipak**](gm_glossary.md#pekipak-sagoya) — wagon inventor, the "Rodential Respirator" guy.

**Friendly-but-suspicious**
- [**Prismatic Colvi**](gm_glossary.md#prismatic-colvi) — high priest of the [Temple of Many Colors](gm_glossary.md#temple-of-many-colors). *(Secret cultist — recurring villain in Act 3. Track how PCs treat him here.)*
- [**The Indigo Stranger**](gm_glossary.md#indigo-stranger) / [**Zanaya**](gm_glossary.md#zanaya) — disguised [indigo dragon](gm_glossary.md#indigo-dragon) [**X'na'zed**](gm_glossary.md#xnazed). Story-helper if PCs get stuck.

**Antagonists**
- [**Melar Kan**](gm_glossary.md#melar-kan) — leader of the [**Iron Eels**](gm_glossary.md#iron-eels) pirate gang. Ch 1 mini-boss.
- [**Squotan Malardy**](gm_glossary.md#squotan-malardy) — Melar Kan's traitor first mate (offscreen — stole the [sapphire scale](gm_glossary.md#sapphire-scale), sold it in [Jakapo Town](gm_glossary.md#jakapo-town)).
- [**Cult of the Shivering Behemoth**](gm_glossary.md#cult-of-the-shivering-behemoth) — looming threat, real reveal in Act 3.
